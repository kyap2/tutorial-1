Buyer's guide to Kei Cars!

What is a Kei Car?

A kei car is a small, fuel-efficient vehicle designed specifically for the Japanese market. These compact cars are popular for their ease of parking, affordability, and unique tax benefits. They come in various styles, including minivans, hatchbacks, and sports cars.

How much do they cost?

Kei cars are generally more affordable than larger vehicles, often ranging from a few thousand to tens of thousands of dollars, depending on factors like age, condition, and specific model. Their compact size and fuel efficiency contribute to lower ownership costs, including reduced fuel expenses and lower insurance premiums.

What models are there?

Kei trucks are small commercial vehicles designed for transporting goods. They are perfect for small businesses and individuals who need to transport goods efficiently. Despite their compact size, they can carry surprisingly large loads.

K-vans are a popular type of Kei car known for their spacious interiors, making them ideal for families and those needing extra cargo space. Despite their compact exterior, they offer surprising interior roominess. These versatile vehicles can be used for daily commuting, weekend getaways, or even light commercial purposes.

Where to buy?

Explore online auctions, dealerships, or private sellers to find Kei cars.
